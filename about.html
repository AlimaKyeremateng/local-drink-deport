Our Ghanaian Heritage

Preserving tradition through authentic beverages since 2010

Our Journey

Founded in Accra by three childhood friends, Taste of Ghana began as a small street stall sharing homemade Sobolo with our community. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a movement to preserve Ghana's rich drink heritage.

Over the past decade, we've traveled to every region of Ghana, learning traditional recipes from elders and mastering techniques passed down through generations. Each drink in our collection tells a story of our ancestors and the land we cherish.

Our Mission

We exist to:

  • Preserve authentic Ghanaian drink recipes
  • Support local farmers and producers
  • Educate new generations about our culinary heritage
  • Innovate while respecting tradition

Every bottle we sell helps fund our community programs teaching traditional brewing methods to young Ghanaians.
